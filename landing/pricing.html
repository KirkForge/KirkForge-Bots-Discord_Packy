Frequently asked

What does "one-time license" actually mean?

You pay once. The license file is signed and you keep using it for the lifetime of the product version you bought. Updates within the same major version are included.

What happens when the support period ends?

The bot keeps running. You lose access to the signed update channel and to priority support tickets. Renew at any time to re-enable both.

Can I move the license to a new machine?

Yes. The license is bound to your organization, not to hardware or a specific Discord server. Drop license.json on the new box, restart, done.

Does Packy work with my LLM provider?

Out of the box: Claude (Anthropic) and MiniMax. Bring your own key. The cognition layer is provider-agnostic; adding OpenAI / Ollama is a thin adapter.

What about VAT / sales tax?

EU and UK buyers see prices including VAT at checkout (Stripe Tax handles this). Outside the EU, the price you see is the price you pay.
